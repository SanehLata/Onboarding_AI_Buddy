## Recurring Meetings

### All-Engineering

| Meeting | Cadence | Time | Location |
|---|---|---|---|
| Engineering All Hands | Monthly (last Thursday) | 3:00 PM – 4:00 PM EST | Zoom |
| Tech Radar Review | Quarterly | 2:00 PM – 3:30 PM EST | Zoom |
| Architecture Guild | Bi-weekly (Wednesday) | 1:00 PM – 2:00 PM EST | Zoom |

### Team-Level (standard across all teams)

| Meeting | Cadence | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Daily Standup | Monday – Friday, 9:30 AM EST | Progress updates, blockers |
| Sprint Planning | Every 2 weeks (Monday) | Plan the upcoming sprint |
| Sprint Retrospective | Every 2 weeks (Friday) | What went well, what to improve |
| Team 1:1 with Manager | Weekly | Career, feedback, unblocking |
| Backlog Refinement | Weekly (Wednesday) | Groom and estimate tickets |

### New Joiner Specific

| Meeting | Cadence | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Onboarding Check-in | Daily for first 2 weeks | Progress check with manager |
| Buddy Sync | Weekly for first 30 days | Casual catch-up with your senior buddy |
| 30-Day Review | Once at day 30 | Formal onboarding review with manager |
| 90-Day Review | Once at day 90 | Performance and growth conversation |

## Escalation Paths

| Situation | Who to Contact |
|---|---|
| Blocked on a task | Your onboarding buddy first, then your manager |
| System access not provisioned | Raise a ServiceNow ticket, CC your manager |
| Production incident | `#incidents` Slack channel immediately |
| HR or personal issue | Your manager or `hr@techcorp.com` |
| Security concern | `security@techcorp.com` — do NOT post in Slack |
